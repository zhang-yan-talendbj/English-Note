Congratulations! That's all! You have just learned all the very basics of SuperMemo!

Now you will need to trust the program for some time before you can see the effects of your work. Your trust will grow if you know how SuperMemo works (see: Principles of SuperMemo at supermemo.com).

Use Learn button daily to make sure you do not forget what you have learned. Use Add new (Alt+A) to add new questions and answers.

Do not forget to back-up your learning material from time to time. Use Ctrl+Shift+C (File : Copy collection) to copy your material to some location on your hard disk. Or better yet: on a DVD-R or a flash drive. Remember that you have two B-choices: Back up or Be sorry! Please take a minute to read: How to back up your learning material in SuperMemo.

To learn more advanced options of SuperMemo, visit 25 Steps to SuperMemo Mastery at supermemo.com.
