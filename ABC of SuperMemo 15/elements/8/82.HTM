To convert a sentence into a cloze do the following:

  1. Select a meaningful keyword in the sentence (e.g. by double-clicking it with the mouse)
  2. Press Alt+Z, or
    click the Z-icon button at the top of this window, or
    choose Reading : Remember cloze on the context menu (right-click), or
    click any of Z-icons on the Read toolbar (see the picture on the right)

To see the newly created cloze question, press Alt+Left or choose the back button ()
